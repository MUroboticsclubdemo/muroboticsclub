MU ROBO FEST   2.0

Our mission is to build a progressive platform for Robotics enthusiasts by active participation in research, training and contest. We do it by organizing various programs, workshops and competitions that help us create connections among students, teachers and employers.
HALL OF FAME

Objectives

  • To build an interactive and supportive platform of Embedded Systems, Robotics and Information Technology integrating students, faculty and corporate individuals.
  • To foster interest in robotics through training and contests.
  • To create motivation for research on Embedded Systems, Robotics and Information Technology.
  • To represent UIU on national and global grounds by organizing and participating in various competitions, research endeavors and innovations relevant to the fields of Embedded Systems, Robotics and Information Technology.
  • To cultivate favorable relationships with proximate communities.


Services

  • Provide long-term and short-term workshops and training programs.
  • Organize and participate in intra and inter level competitions on both national and international grounds.
  • Conduct research projects related to Embedded Systems, Robotics and Information Technology under a Faculty(s) of the University.
  • Organize seminars and outreach program both in own campus and other institutions to promote Embedded Systems, Robotics and Information Technology.
  • Provide mentorship for student projects with approval from the Mentor(s).
  • Provide recommendations for employment inside and outside the University.
  • Provide organizational support to the University when requested.
